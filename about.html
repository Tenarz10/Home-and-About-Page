About

Founded in 1993. Books n' Beans had its humble beginnings as a resale store for outdated college textbooks. As its cult status in the community grew, so did customer demand for reliable source of caffeine. Thus, the current iteration of Books n' Beans was born.

We're open 7 days a week, offering impeccable coffee, fine pasteries, and the most esotric of publications.
